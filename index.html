Services

Dialysis

Being a nephrologist, I specialize in caring for your kidneys and ensuring they function at their best. Whether it's managing kidney diseases or providing life-saving dialysis treatments, I am here to support you every step of the way. With my expertise, I can offer comprehensive dialysis services, helping to cleanse your blood and remove waste when your kidneys can't do it on their own. Your well-being is my priority, and I'm committed to providing you with the highest quality care to ensure you lead a healthy and fulfilling life.

Kidney diseases

Kidneys are the vital organs responsible for filtering waste and maintaining balance in your body. Whether it's kidney-related diseases, glomerular issues affecting their filtering units, or electrolyte imbalances throwing off your body's harmony, I'm here to provide expert care and support. With my dedication and knowledge, you can trust that your kidney health is in capable hands.

Expert in managing diabetes

As an M.D in General Medicine, I possess a comprehensive understanding of how diabetes can impact various organs, including the kidneys. My expertise allows me to effectively manage diabetes and its related complications, such as kidney disease. Through precise diagnosis, personalized treatment plans, and ongoing monitoring, I strive to optimize my patients' health and quality of life, providing them with the support and guidance needed to navigate the complexities of living with diabetes.

Kidney transplant

I specialize in the intricate world of kidney health, including the life-changing realm of kidney transplants. With extensive experience in overseeing approximately 250 successful kidney transplants, I offer expertise and compassionate care to patients in need of this vital procedure. My dedication to guiding individuals through every step of the transplant journey ensures the best possible outcomes, providing hope and renewed quality of life to those facing kidney-related challenges.
